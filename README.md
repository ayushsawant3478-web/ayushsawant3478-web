## 🧠 About Me

Fresh IT graduate from Somaiya Vidyavihar University (Mumbai) with a B.Sc. in Information Technology and a minor in Cybersecurity. I operate at the intersection of security engineering, IT audit, and compliance — with hands-on depth in threat detection, identity and access management, and incident response workflows.

My flagship project, **Trackify**, is a self-built financial security platform that implements JWT authentication, OAuth 2.0, RBAC, session monitoring, behavioral anomaly detection, and audit logging from scratch — not a tutorial clone.

On the platform side, I've completed 10 TryHackMe rooms (Top 40% globally, 5 badges), earned Google Cloud Skills Boost Silver League (6 labs), and am actively working through CompTIA Security+ SY0-701.
